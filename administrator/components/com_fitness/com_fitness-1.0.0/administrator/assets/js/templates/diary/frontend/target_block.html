 TOTAL DAILY WATER TARGET (TDWT)

Your TDWT is the amount of water your trainer has estimated you need to consume on a daily
basis. Remember, this is only an estimate. The actual amount required can be influenced by
other factors such as more exercise on a particular day, illness and fluid content in dietary intake.
Pregnant and breast feeding women need to drink additional fluids to stay hydrated.
